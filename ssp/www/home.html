COMMITTED TO QUALITY

When you choose SSP as your packaging partner, you choose quality you can trust. Our products undergo rigorous quality checks to ensure they meet the highest industry standards.

01 Box Compression Test

It helps determine stacking strength and suitability for palletized storage. This test ensures boxes won’t collapse under the weight of other boxes during transportation or warehousing.

02 Burst Strength Test

It indicates resistance to internal pressure from the packed contents. This test is relevant for products that might exert force on the box from within, like heavy or sharp objects.

03 Edge Crush Test

It assesses the box’s ability to withstand vertical compression during handling and transportation. This test is crucial for boxes that are frequently lifted, moved, or stored vertically.

03 GSM Test

It provides an indication of the overall thickness and strength of the cardboard. Higher GSM typically translates to a stronger board, but it’s important to consider the specific application and balance cost with performance.

Manual Production Approach

We started our journey in this industry using the manual production method which is labour intensive and includes die cutting, boxing, folding, etc. The reason we continue to use this method is because we believe in giving back to the society in terms of a living for the staff and also because the Minimum Order is very flexible here thereby catering to low orders.

Technology-Driven Production Approach

In keeping the heavy demands, we chose to use state of the art machines to cater to the bulky orders. But, we still offer flexibility to the customers in terms of Minimum Order. Here, the orders are customized to individual orders.
